ABOUT US

Now Listen Here is a live storytelling series. We welcome this space for local community members to share their stories without the pressure of competition. Stories are true, as remembered by the storytellers and are presented without notes! Join us at an open mic show to share your story!

NEWS

We are excited to announce that as of February 12th, we will be expanding to The Lily Pad in Cambridge! Over the past several years, our community has expanded greatly so it was time that we did too. Stay tuned for more information about upcoming events at the Lilypad, workshops, and more.
